How long has it been since your most recent certification? As most of you know the dive industry is constantly evolving and with this evolution comes new information. This information is paramount to safety in the water. Some of the info in this section you learned during your training and some you may not have learned. So sit back and refresh your knowledge.

Traveling the World Can Be Pretty Complex and Stressfull

You may ask why is travel in this section? I feel the more you know about a place you are diving the more relaxed you will be. In my opinion a relaxed diver tends to be a safer diver. You may not be able to completely eliminate stress but you can reduce it by having a better knowledge of where you are going.
